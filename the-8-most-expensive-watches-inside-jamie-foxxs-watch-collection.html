The 8 Most Expensive Watches Inside Jamie Foxx's Watch Collection

Quick Links

  • Rolex President Day-Date ($52,000)
  • Jaeger LeCoultre Grande Reverso Calendar ($14,375)
  • IWC Portugieser Chronograph ($10,500)
  • Rolex Submariner Ceramic Bezel ($12,000)
  • Custom Hublot Watch ($50,000)
  • Rolex Datejust Pavé Diamonds ($16,361)
  • Cartier Santos De Cartier ($70,000)
  • Pristine ‘Chandelier’ ($450,000)

Growing up in a strict Baptist house and with his grandmother, Jamie Foxx was learning piano when he was just five years of age. A talented musician from the start, he worked hard at school and excelled in sports and academics to earn a scholarship for studying music. Foxx was also an entertainer at heart and first began his stand-up comedy career and released multiple specials. He soon pivoted toward making comedy movies and using his musical talents to release music. The early 1990s and 2000s saw Foxx dominate both industries and even win an Oscar for his lead performance as the titular character in Ray. The artist amassed a hefty $170 million fortune through his music and movies.

Growing up in a small home, Jamie Foxx knew the value of his hard work and invested his money in multiple ventures. The actor is also known to make expensive real estate and automobile purchases. He is often seen wearing a blingy watch on his wrist that costs a lot more than a few hundred thousand dollars. Let’s take a look at the expensive watch collection of Jamie Foxx.

Rolex President Day-Date ($52,000)

The Rolex President Day-Date in yellow gold has been a staple on Foxx’s wrist since 2017. The actor was spotted wearing the watch during his performance hosting the Beat Shazam event with his daughter Corinne in 2018 and the Fox Upfront Event in 2019. According to the Swiss Watch Expo, the timepiece is from the 18-carat yellow gold collection and features a champagne dial with diamonds as hour markers. The artist also uses the formal watch as an everyday accessory.

Jaeger LeCoultre Grande Reverso Calendar ($14,375)

When adapting a musical into a movie, Annie was a highly-anticipated release that hit theaters in 2014. Foxx played the lead role of William Stacks, Annie’s adopted father, and as a wealthy politician, the actor was often spotted wearing high-end outfits. Foxx sported a Jaeger LeCoultre Grande Reverso Calendar during and after the filming. The timepiece was made with a yellow-gold bezel and a dark leather strap.

IWC Portugieser Chronograph ($10,500)

Jamie Foxx had owned the IWC Portugieser Chronograph since 2005 when he was shooting Miami Vice, which was released a year later. The film collaborated with IWC to promote its watches and wore the timepiece throughout the shooting. During the press tour, he switched to a silver dial version with a sapphire glass coating on both sides, as noted by IWC. The watchmaker even released a limited-edition Miami Vice Portugieser during the time, with only 50 units available.

Rolex Submariner Ceramic Bezel ($12,000)

He prefers to go with a simple yet elegant watch at events and parties and chooses a Rolex Submariner. His ceramic bezel watch with a silver bracelet is a popular model amongst many celebrities in Hollywood. For safety reasons, his sleek watch has a black dial and a waterproof Oyster case.

Custom Hublot Watch ($50,000)

Watches have often symbolized special occasions, and Hublot gifted the actor a custom watch while attending award ceremonies after his breakthrough performance in Ray. As mentioned by The Richest, he wore the same timepiece while collecting an Oscar for Best Actor. The pink gold watch was set with diamonds and an $11,500 rubber strap. Foxx also wore it for other award ceremonies.

Rolex Datejust Pavé Diamonds ($16,361)

One of the most eye-catching watches in Jamie Foxx’s collection is the Rolex Datejust encrusted with Pavé Diamonds. The timepiece is iced-out from each direction and comes with a royal blue dial with gold hour markers. The gold numerals are encrusted with diamond markers, while the bezel has small round diamonds as a classic finishing touch.

Cartier Santos De Cartier ($70,000)

A watch with a deep history, the Cartier Santos De Cartier was first designed in 1904 with its signature exposed screws and curved horns, as stated by Cartier. Foxx was first spotted wearing the watch in August 2021 when he posted a photo on Instagram. His watch is a two-tone model with a combination of yellow gold and diamonds. The dials are iced-out Roman numerals, and the timepiece has a diamond bracelet.

Pristine ‘Chandelier’ ($450,000)

Jamie Foxx went complete custom when he ordered a watch from Pristine Watches in London. Spotted on his wrist in 2021, his watch is made from 18-carat white gold and features a complete set of emerald-cut diamonds worth 97 carats. The watch does not have any hour makers on the dials but two simple blue dials to showcase the time.

Jamie Foxx has amassed a stunning watch collection with every passing year and project. While his initial watches were simple yet elegant, he has recently switched to iced-out models with custom finishes that stand out. The actor’s love for watches remains ever-growing, and he will be spotted wearing more stunning watches on his social media account.
