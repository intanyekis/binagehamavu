Taylor Swift Has 'Already Written' Love Songs For Travis Kelce But Won't Share Them With Fans

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift has written love songs for Travis Kelce, drawing inspiration from their relationship and falling in love together.
  • Although the songs won't be released or performed live, they hold a special place in Taylor's heart as a personal outlet for her emotions.
  • Taylor and Travis' relationship is serious and filled with love and support, with both families approving of their romance.

Taylor Swift is an incredibly prolific artist, and one of the reasons is because she finds inspiration in the people around her. Falling in love is a very meaningful thing for everybody, but when it comes to musicians, they usually get the nice bonus of getting inspired. Now that Taylor and Travis Kelce have been together for a while, fans have been wondering whether there will be new songs about their romance.

Taylor Swift’s Relationship With Travis Kelce Is Drastically Different From Her Past Famous Romances

There are a lot of unique things about Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's fascinating relationship.

The simple answer is that, yes, there will be. In fact, they already exist. As their relationship gets increasingly serious, the superstar has been writing songs about Travis and the experience of falling in love with him. But sadly, fans won't get to hear them. At least not for a while.

Taylor Swift Has Written Songs For Travis Kelce

Like for most artists, love has been one of the main sources of inspiration for Taylor Swift. The singer is known for using her music to work through her emotions, both positive and negative, so it makes sense that, now that she's in a new, loving relationship, those feelings influence her art.

Taylor Swift is now on her Eras tour, and working towards the release of her new album, The Tortured Poets Department, but she reportedly still had time to write love songs for Travis Kelce.

"Taylor has already written songs about Travis," a source recently shared. "She's written at least two songs. They have to do with their love story and falling in love with him."

How Many Songs Has Taylor Swift Written?

Taylor Swift has penned plenty of hits, but how many songs has she actually written in total?

Before readers get excited, those songs are unlikely to be featured on her upcoming album, and she won't play them live either. Writing songs is an outlet for Taylor Swift, but that doesn't mean she releases everything she writes. And these songs are "very personal" and not the kind of thing she wants to share with the world.

"She likely won't share them with anyone. They're very special. Songs are like poetry to her. Just like most people journal, Taylor writes lyrics. Her relationship with Travis has inspired her," the source added.

Taylor And Travis' Relationship Is Getting Serious

Things seem to be getting serious between Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, despite the fact that they haven't been together for that long. Every time the two of them are seen together, they look hopelessly in love, and even their families have revealed that they are very happy together.

Both are very busy and successful people, but they still make time to support each other as much as they can. On at least three separate occasions, they have traveled around the world to be with each other during important moments (Travis has traveled to Argentina and Australia to watch her perform, and Taylor jetted from Japan to Las Vegas to cheer him on at the Super Bowl), which proves how serious they are about each other.

"This is just the beginning for them as a couple and many milestones are coming their way. Taylor and Travis make time for each other because they want to," a source shared. And not just that, their families also adore each other.

"They both take so much pride in the importance of family and love bringing their families and close-knit circles together," the source added. "They are genuine, honest, loving, and forthright with each other across the board. They both continually encourage one another to be the best that they can be."
