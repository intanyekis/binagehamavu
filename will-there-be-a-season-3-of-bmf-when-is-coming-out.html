Will there be a Season 3 of BMF? When is Coming Out? The Talks Today

The Black Mafia Family 

BMF is a crime drama TV series that premiered on Starz on September 26, 2021. It revolves around the Black Mafia Family, a criminal organization engaged in drug trafficking and money laundering. The series was quickly renewed for a second season, which began on January 6, 2023, and then for a third season in January 2023. The show is produced by Lionsgate Television and G-Unit Films and Television Inc. and is executive produced by Curtis “50 Cent” Jackson, who developed the series for Starz. The network announced the renewal of the series for the second season just four days after the show premiered in September 2021.

Will there be a season 3 of BMF?

Following the successful debut of BMF’s second season, which premiered on January 6 and ranked #1 on STARZ, the network has announced that the crime drama series will be renewed for a third season. The show, inspired by the true story of the Black Mafia Family of the 1980s, revolves around brothers Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory (played by Demetrius Flenory Jr.) and Terry “Southwest T” Flenory (played by Da’Vinchi) as they rise to become one of the most prominent crime families in America. The cast also includes Russell Hornsby, Michole Briana White, Eric Kofi-Abrefa, Myles Truitt, Steve Harris, and La La Anthony.

Kathryn Busby, President of Original Programming for STARZ, expressed excitement for the continuation of the Flenory brothers’ story and praised the show’s elevated, gritty storytelling that captures the essence of Detroit. The series is executive produced by Curtis “50 Cent” Jackson and showrunner Randy Huggins and produced through Jackson’s G-Unit Film and Television in association with Lionsgate Television for STARZ. Other executive producers on the series include Heather Zuhlke, Anthony Wilson, and Anne Clements. Kathryn Tyus-Adair and Erin Conroy are overseeing the series on behalf of STARZ and Lionsgate, respectively.

BMF Plot 

BMF is based on the real-life tale of two brothers who grew up in southwest Detroit during the late 1980s and went on to establish one of the most significant crime families in America’s history. With Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory’s captivating leadership, Terry “Southwest T” Flenory’s business acumen, and their shared vision that expanded beyond drug trafficking and into the world of hip hop, the brothers would become legendary on a global scale. However, their profound belief in family loyalty would also be the foundation of their partnership’s success and their eventual falling out. The show explores themes of love, deceit, and ruthless capitalism in pursuit of the American dream. Filming for BMF began in January 2021 and took place in both Atlanta and Detroit.
