Molt is an American YouTuber who is best known for the gaming videos that he uploads to his YouTube channel titled MOLT. It includes gaming details on several multiplayer strategy games like Clash Royale and Battle Royale. Molt’s secondary channel titled MoltPlays has videos of RPG and FPS related tutorial, reveals, and in-depth details about games like Roblox, Fortnite, Overwatch, and the Call of Duty game series. On the contrary, Molt also has 2 other channels, one of which is titled The Holts which is shared with his wife and consist of videos based on their travel and everyday vlogs. The second is an eponymous channel to which he uploads the video series of ‘A Year in Marriage’. He has also amassed a huge fan following with more than 2 million subscribers on his primary YouTube channel, more than 250k subscribers on his secondary channel, and more than 300k followers on Twitter.
