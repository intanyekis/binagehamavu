In 2007, the Swedish actress rose to prominence in the Danish film ‘Daisy Diamond’ as a disturbed adolescent parent. She then starred as Lisbeth Salander in the 2009 film ‘The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo,’ which catapulted her to international renown.

The film was an adaptation of Stieg Larsson’s first novel of the same name, as well as the Millennium series of novels. In 2010, she appeared in another Swedish drama, ‘Beyond,’ directed by Pernilla August.
