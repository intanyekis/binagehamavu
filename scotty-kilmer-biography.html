Scotty Kilmer Bio (Wiki)

Who is Scotty Kilmer?

Scotty Kilmer an American web star and Auto mechanic. He is popular for his amazing Automotive content on his YouTube channel.

He is also an author and wrote a book titled Everyone’s Guide to Buying a Used Car and Car Maintenance in 1994.

Scotty Kilmer- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 02 Oct 1953 and he was born in Niagara Falls, New York, the United States as Scott Michael Kilmer.

The name of his mother and his father are hidden. But his late grandfather, Elmer was a chief mechanic.

Scotty’s ancestry is Caucasian and as per his academics, he went to York University and the University of Illinois.

From the latter, Scotty Kilmer achieved his master’s degree in anthropology in 1977.

Scotty Kilmer- Professional Life, Career

Scotty Kilmer has worked as a host for a show named Car Talk Host. It was aired on CBS TV and he was with the show from 1995 till 2007.

As a YouTuber

He started his YouTube journey in Aug 2007 and as of now has gained millions of subscribers. He uploads Car Talk Q&As and car maintenance tutorials for his over 4.32 million subscribers.

Scotty also shares videos related to mechanical problems, and opinions on vehicle engineering, and provides solutions to low-profile problems.

Some of his most-watched posts are Here’s Why I Want to Buy This Lexus for My New Daily Driver, This Car is Better Than a Lexus, Doing This Will Make Your Engine Run Better (I Guarantee It), and more.
