Wildfire erupts near Cascade: Boise National Forest responding

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — A wildfire has broken out roughly 10 miles southeast of Cascade, Idaho. The Cascade Fire Department shared to social media that appropriate resources have been notified and the Boise National Forest is currently in the air and deploying resources to the incident.
