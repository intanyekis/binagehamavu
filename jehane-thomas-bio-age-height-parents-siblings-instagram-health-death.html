Jehane Thomas Bio; Age, Height, Parents, Siblings, Instagram, Health, Death

Late Jehane Thomas was a TikTok star who hailed from Doncaster, United Kingdom.

The mother of two became famous when she began posting video contents about her daily life on TikTok.

So far,  Thomas has garnered more than 66,700 followers and 1.2 million viewers on the platform.

Jehane Thomas Health

Thomas’ health challenges are no news to her followers, as she had previously disclosed that she had been diagnosed with optic neuritis.

Optic neuritis is caused by “inflammation of the optic nerve, which transmits messages from the eye to the brain.”

Thomas had also disclosed that she had been scheduled for a surgery.

‘I can’t lift my head up without wanting to be sick and I’m unable to walk, I need to be wheeled everywhere. That’s how bad this pain is,’ she wrote in a video caption.

A week after she was discharged from the hospital,  she complained of migraines and passed on.

Jehane Thomas Death

Thomas passed away on March 17, 2023, after suffering from series of migraines.

Thomas had shared a “tearful video” about terrible migraine pains, just two days before her demise.

She is survived by her husband and two children.
