Scott Studwell Age and Wikipedia Explored

Scott Studwell, a former professional American football player, is 67 years old. “Stud” was his nickname.

Scott Studwell has been one of the Vikings’ most devoted employees.

He was a part of the Vikings’ 25th and 40th Anniversary seasons teams, and in 2010, he was designated one of the 50 greatest Vikings.

After retiring, he assisted the Vikings in drafting 242 players between 1991 and 2019, scouting legendary players like Randy Moss, Adrian Peterson, and Harrison Smith.
