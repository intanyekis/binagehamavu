Is Denis Lawson Related to Ewan McGregor? The Star Wars Family Connection You Didnt Know About

Star Wars is one of the most popular and influential franchises in the history of cinema, spanning over four decades and nine main films, as well as numerous spin-offs, animated series, video games, books, and comics. Denis Lawson is a Scottish actor and director who was born in Glasgow in 1947. Who is Ewan McGregor?

Ewan McGregor is a Scottish actor who was born in Perth in 1971. He is best known for his role as Obi-Wan Kenobi, a Jedi master who trained Anakin Skywalker and later Luke Skywalker in the Star Wars prequel trilogy. He appeared in all three films: The Phantom Menace (1999), Attack of the Clones (2002), and Revenge of the Sith (2005).
