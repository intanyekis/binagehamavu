T.I HARRIS APPOLOGISED TO HIS DAUGHTER

There was a storm of backlash against T.I. over what most considered a gross violation of his daughter's privacy as a person and as a woman.

Jada Pinkett Smith:

“Let me go set this record straight, I never said I was in any exam room, that is an assumption, that is a falsity. I never said that it was being done present day as an 18-year-old…and I never said that her mother wasn’t present…Her mom was present every time.”

He also added that he had no idea the subject would be so sensitive and explosive and that the whole experience had been an education in sensibility towards his daughters and wife.
