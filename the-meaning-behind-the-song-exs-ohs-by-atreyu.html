Atreyu drew inspiration from personal experiences and relationships. The band members channeled their own emotions and translated them into this captivating song. It serves as a cathartic outlet for them and listeners alike, as they navigate the complexities of love and heartache.

Atreyu wanted to capture the turbulent aftermath of a breakup and the emotional turmoil that comes with it.
