Meet Jordan Foxworthy, the eldest daughter of Jeff Foxworthy.

Jordan Foxworthy is a member, a fundraiser, of the American non-profit making organization Compassion International. The organization helps to release children from poverty in Jesus's name. Foxworthy previously served as a campus administrator and ambassador of the Atlanta Mission.

Besides the humanitarian works, Jordan is widely popular as the beloved daughter of famous American entertainment personality Jeff Foxworthy. Moreover, Jordan also works as an administrative assistant for her father's official website Foxworthy Outdoors.

Early Life

Jordan was born in November 1991, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. She is the eldest daughter of Jeff Foxworthy and his wife, Pamela Gregg. Jordan has a sister named Juliane Foxworthy.

Jordan's younger sister Juliane who graduated from Auburn University is happily married to her boyfriend-turned-husband, Brendan Corley. Juliane and Brendan are also expecting their first child, a son.  

Concerning Jordan's educational details, she is an alumnus of the University of Southern California (USC). Similarly, Jordan received a degree in Sociology, Film, and African Studies from The University of Georgia.

Likewise, Jordan earned a certificate in social justice and Resilient Leadership from Kilns College and The Seattle School of Theology & Psychology, respectively. As per Jordan's Linkedin profile, she studies Film Producing at UCLA Extensions.

Celebrity Father: Jeff Foxworthy

The 62-years-old Jeffrey Marshall Foxworthy, aka Jeff, is a widely popular actor, stand-up comedian, radio/TV personality, and author as well. He is identified as a member of the comedy troupes Blue Collar Comedy Tour.

The Grammy Award-nominated Jeff has worked in the shows such as The Jeff Foxworthy Show, The American Bible Challenge, Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grade? etc. Similarly, he has provided his voice for animated films like Racing Stripes, The Smurfs, The Fox and the Hound 2, etc.

Likewise, Foxworthy has also released albums like You Might Be a Rednick If, Games Rednecks Paly, Crank It Up: The Music Album, Big Funny, Have Your Loved Ones Spayed or Neutered, and Totally Committed. 

Parent's (Jeff & Pamela)'s Marital Bond

The actor Jeff and his long time supportive, caring, loving wife Pamela are one of the power couples all over the media. Foxworthy first met Gregg in Atlanta. After the first encounter, they shared a good bond and soon start their romantic affair. 

Pamela and Jeff tied the wedding knot on September 18, 1985. Since that day, they are sharing a healthy bond as a perfect married partner. They spend their vacation and attend numerous public events together. 

As of 2020, Jeff and his beloved wife Pamela are celebrating their 35-years (more than three decades) marriage anniversary merrily with zero signs of separation. 

Jordan's Relationship Status

The 29-years-old Jordan has not mentioned her current relationship status. She has not declared whether she is living a single life or is dating someone privately.

It seems like Jordan currently enjoys her single life. She has also not shared any romantic pictures with love captions on her social media post. At the moment, Jordan is busy with her studies and career. So, this might be the reason behind her singleness. 

Professional Works

From May 2009 to July 2009, Jordan served as a guest panelist speaker for BigStuf Camps. Similarly, she served as a Production Assistant for the Fox game show Are You Smarter Than A Fifth Grader from June to July 2009. 

Likewise, from June 2011 to July 2012, Jordan was a co-host of the Empower Radio program Fresh Forward, along with her friend Sarah Politis. Moreover, Jordan worked as an assistant producer for the video content studio Green Tricycle Studios.

Furthermore, Foxworthy served as an associate producer for the documentary film Imba Means Sing. Similarly, she was an ambassador for Atlanta Mission. As per their official site, Atlanta Mission is a "provider of services to homeless men, women, and children." Since October 2020, Jordan is working for the non-profit company Compassion International.

Net Worth

Jordan has an estimated net worth of around $300 thousand, which she accumulated from her various jobs. Besides earning a good sum of wealth, Jordan is also involved in various philanthropic works. 

As of 2020, Jordan's popular father, Jeff, owns a fortune of around $100 million. The primary source of earning for Jeff is from acting, hosting, and many others.
