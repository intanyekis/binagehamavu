American actress, Hailee Steinfeld has an estimated net worth of $8 million. Hailee Steinfeld began her professional career in 2007, at the age of eleven. After appearing in various commercials and television projects, she was cast in the film, “True Grit”, for which she was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress and a BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role.

Hailee Steinfeld was only 13 when she played obstinate 19th-century teen Mattie Ross in the western True Grit. It was her first major role, and it won her a nomination as best supporting actress in that year's Oscars and Screen Actors Guild awards. She had bit parts in the TV series Back To You (2007, with Kelsey Grammer) and Sons of Tucson (2010), and also appeared in a national Kmart TV ad, before beating out a reported 15,000 other auditioners for the role in True Grit. In long brown pigtails and a gray felt hat, Steinfeld played an Arkansas teen who sets out to avenge the death of her father and was praised by critics for holding her own with her veteran Oscar-winning co-stars Jeff Bridges and Matt Damon.

Hailee Steinfeld was born on December 11, 1996, in Tarzana, Los Angeles, California, United States.
