How did Xia Vigor start her Professional Career?

Xia first appeared on television in the show ‘It’s Showtime.’ This Philippine noontime variety show was broadcast on the ABS-CBN network. She was the first grand prize winner of its ‘Mini-Me 2’ segment, in which she played a miniature Selena Gomez. As a result, Xia was promoted to co-host of the ‘Xia and Kuys segment on the show.

During this segment, she co-hosted the show with Filipino comedian Vhong Navarro and Filipino-American actor Billy Crawford. Similarly, she rose to prominence after appearing in the 2017 season of the kid’s reality show “Your Face Sounds Familiar Kids.” In this celebrity kids’ singing and impersonation competition, Xia impersonated American pop singer Taylor Swift.

Similarly, Xia has appeared in several films, the first of which was in 2017. In the Filipino fantasy horror comedy film ‘Mang Kepweng Returns,’ she made her acting debut.
