Who is the Husband of American Comedian Turned Actress, Amy Schumer? Know About Her Child and Her Ne

If you love watching, stand-up comedy then, Amy Schumer must not be a new name to you. Amy is a famous American stand-up comedian and a actress. She started her career in the late 1990s as a comedian and later entered into acting. 

Amy Schumer was born as Amy Beth Schumer on June 1, 1981, in Manhattan, New York City. She is the daughter of Sandra Jane Schumer and her husband, Gordan David Schumer. Her father owned a baby-furniture company, and her mother worked at home. Schumer's father is the second cousin of U.S Senator, Chuck Schumer. Amy graduated from South Side High School in 1999. 

Married Life of Amy Schumer

The 39, years old comedian is living a happy life with her husband. Amy is married to her boyfriend turned husband, Chris Fischer. The love birds dated for sometime before tying their knots. 

Amy walked down the wedding aisle on February 13, 2018, in Malibu, California. They had a private wedding ceremony. Only a few members from both the families and some close friends attended the ceremony. Amy Schumer surprised fans in February when she announced that, "Yup," she had tied the knot with Chris Fischer.

The love birds first met each other in 2017, when he came over to Schumer's vacation home to cook for her. After receiving Molly's blessing, the pair officially became an item, and things moved quickly after that, Schumer recalled.

A few months after they began dating, Fischer proposed, and Schumer said she took three to four days to plan the wedding, including picking out a dress.
