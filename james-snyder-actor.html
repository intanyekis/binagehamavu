James Snyder is an American stage, film, and television actor and singer who has been cast in a number of stage productions such as Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, Rock of Ages, Fanny, Casa Valentina, If/Then, Johnny Baseball, Grand Hotel, Sneaux!, and Cry-Baby. In addition to that, he has also appeared in on-screen roles like Gil Berridge in The Good Wife, Jason Harrington in The Suite Life of Zack & Cody, Jeremy Leigh in The Gingerdead Man, David in Pretty Persuasion, Zac in Married to the Kellys, Breck Mulligan in Without a Trace, Hunter Ahearn in CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, David in Drop Dead Diva, Brad Adams in Rizzoli and Isles, and Malcolm Festes in She’s the Man.

    James Snyder Facts

  • He made his first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of David “Dave” in the 2005 black comedy film, Pretty Persuasion, starring Evan Rachel Wood, James Woods, Ron Livingston, Elisabeth Harnois, and Jane Krakowski.
  • On March 20, 2008, James Snyder released a solo album titled L.A. Curse. It included songs like Somebody Better, Until the Moon Goes Down, Danna, Free, Take One for the Team, Backup, and I Tried.
  • In 2013, James originated the role of Josh in the musical If/Then and he won the 2014 Broadway.com Audience Award for “Favorite Onstage Pair” (w/ Idina Menzel). The role also got him nominated for the 2014 Broadway.com Audience Award for “Favorite Supporting Actor”.
