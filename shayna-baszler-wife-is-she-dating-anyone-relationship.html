Is She Dating Anyone? Bisexual &amp; Girlfriend

Shayna Baszler, the 43-year-old former UFC fighter-turned-wrestler, is openly bisexual. Fans are curious to know if she has a wife or dating someone.

The former two-time NXT Women’s Champion may be bold about her sexuality & her perspective but keeps prying eyes on her personal life at bay.

Moreover, if it is about her dating life, things become less certain. Even though the WWE wrestler admits she’s been in a relationship, she kept details of her partner under wraps.

Born on August 8, 1980, Baszler is an American professional wrestler who performs in the Raw brand of the WWE.

The Sioux Falls native formerly fought as a mixed martial artist in promotions like Elite XC, Strikeforce, Invicta & the UFC, with 26 bouts (15 wins & 11 losses).

Trained under former UFC fighter Josh Barnett, Shayna had 2 fights in the UFC but lost both; to Bethe Correia (in UFC 117) and Amanda Nunes (in UFC Fight Night: Maia vs LaFlare).

After the UFC released her, she went into professional wrestling & climbed from the NXT to the WWE.

There she became a three-time WWE Women’s Tag Team Champion, partnering once with Ronda Rousey & two times with Nia Jax.

Shayna Baszler Wife: Is She Married?

Given that, Shayna is openly bisexual, it comes as no surprise that fans are curious if she has a wife or husband.

However, the WWE diva, who goes by the ring name “The Queen of Spades,” is not married officially as of 2023.

No details of her marriage or husband/wife have come forth into the media to date.

Many are curious to know if she has a secret relationship since she’s been private about the details of her lovers in the past.

Seems all those claims stand with one foot on the grave without any evidence.

But she is already a mother to her pet dog named Rudolph.

On her Instagram, Shayna often shares wrestling-related content, workout videos & her canine pet.

Well, Baszler is not the only proud wrestler from the LGBTQ community to have been open about her sexuality on the WWE platform.

Her fellow wrestler Tegan Nox is currently in a relationship with Aly Deliz; previously, she made highlights with her relationship with a lady named Sierra St. Pierre.

The others on the list of famous LGBTQ wrestlers include Mercedes Martinez, Toni Storm & Sonya Deville.

Came Out As Bisexual In 2013- Who Is Shayna Dating?

Baszler is bold about her sexuality & often mentions her relationship in interviews but keeps the details limited.

During an episode of the podcast MMA Roasted on October 14, 2013, the former UFC fighter came out as bisexual & said that she was in a relationship with a girl at that time.

Regarding the answer to the query if she has dated men & women, Shayna said:

“I am currently seeing someone that happens to be a female, but that’s not to say that I’m not one of those people that would color myself black or white this just happens to be where I’m at right now.”

It’s not known if the girl she was dating at that time is still with her.

As for the details about her relationship in the past & these days, she has switched to the private mode.

Amidst the silence, there are dating rumors swirling about the WWE diva.

The two-time NXT Women’s Champion was once rumored to have a relationship with her real-life friend Ronda Rousey.

But it vanished in thin air shortly after as the latter lives with her husband Travis Browne.

Ultimately, whether Shayna is dating someone or single remains a mystery as she continues to focus on her career in the wrestling industry.
