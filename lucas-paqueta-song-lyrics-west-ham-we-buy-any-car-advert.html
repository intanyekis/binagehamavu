Lucas Paqueta is starting to make a real impact at West Ham.

The Brazilian struggled to initially hit the ground running following his club record £51million switch from Lyon last summer.

But since the turn of the year, Paqueta started to show why West Ham smashed their transfer record to bring him in.

He has registered five goals and six assists in all competitions and fans are hoping he will be the main man in the Europa Conference League Final.

And Paqueta just might be even more motivated to impress against Fiorentina when he hears his new song belt out in Prague.
